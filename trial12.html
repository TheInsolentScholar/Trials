THE 12 TRIALS OF LUCY

TRIAL 12

Driving Lessons

As Heracles faced his final and most daunting labor—capturing Cerberus, guardian of the underworld—you must now confront the challenge of applying your knowledge on the road through practical driving lessons.

Your Task: You must take at least 3 driving lessons.

In this culminating trial, theory meets practice. The Greeks understood that true mastery comes not just from knowledge but from experience and application. Now you must put your understanding of driving to the test under the guidance of an experienced instructor.

Like Heracles descending into the underworld, you venture onto the roads with both trepidation and courage. Each lesson will build your confidence and skill until you are ready to journey independently.

Heracles' final labor required him to enter the underworld and return with Cerberus, the three-headed guard dog. This was considered the most difficult trial because it required not just strength but courage to face the unknown and wisdom to navigate unfamiliar terrain. Similarly, driving lessons require you to venture into the sometimes intimidating world of actual traffic, applying your theoretical knowledge under real conditions. Just as Heracles received guidance from Hermes and Athena for his descent, you have an instructor to guide you through this challenging transition. And just as Heracles emerged triumphant from the underworld, you will emerge from your lessons with new skills that grant you the freedom to travel to distant lands—including Valencia.
