THE 12 TRIALS OF LUCY

The Journey Begins

Greetings, brave Lucy! Like the great heroes of ancient Greece, you stand at the threshold of a legendary quest. These twelve trials will test your courage, wisdom, and determination.

Just as Heracles was tasked by King Eurystheus to complete twelve impossible labors, so too are you challenged to complete these modern trials before your journey to Valencia may commence.

Each challenge will require different skills and mindsets. Some will test your patience, others your creativity, and still others your physical prowess. All will prepare you for the adventures that await in Valencia.

The ancient oracles have foretold that only those who successfully navigate all twelve trials shall be deemed worthy of the sacred journey. The gods of Olympus watch your progress with interest!

Begin your heroic quest with courage in your heart. Document your progress as evidence of your accomplishments. May wisdom guide your path and glory await your triumph!
