THE 12 TRIALS OF LUCY

TRIAL 1

Driving

As Heracles faced the mighty Nemean Lion with courage and determination, so must you confront the first trial on your path to Valencia: mastering the art of driving.

Your Task: Apply for and obtain a provisional driving license through the proper government channels.

The journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step. Before one can master the chariot, one must first obtain permission to learn its ways. Navigate the labyrinth of bureaucracy and emerge victorious with your provisional license in hand.

Document your application process and celebrate when your provisional license arrives - the symbol of your first conquest on this heroic journey.

Just as Heracles needed to understand the invulnerability of the Nemean Lion before defeating it, you must understand the rules of the road before you can master them. The lion's impenetrable hide represents the initially challenging task of learning to drive - but with persistence, you shall overcome.
