THE 12 TRIALS OF LUCY

TRIAL 3

Build an App

As Heracles pursued the swift Ceryneian Hind with golden antlers, so must you chase the elusive art of web creation. This trial demands patience, creativity, and technical skill to capture your vision in digital form.

Your Task: Build a app on lovable or some other similar app creation tool, it must not be boring

The ancient Greeks were master builders, creating temples and monuments that have stood the test of time. Now you must build in the digital realm, crafting a virtual space that others can visit and interact with.

Like the Ceryneian Hind that ran with incredible speed and grace, a well-designed website must load quickly and flow smoothly for its visitors. Your creation should be both functional and aesthetically pleasing.

Heracles tracked the Ceryneian Hind for an entire year before finally capturing it without causing harm. Similarly, building your first website may take time and persistence. The interactive element represents the careful way Heracles had to handle the sacred deer - your site must not just exist but respond to those who visit it, creating a relationship between creator and viewer.
