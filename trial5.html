THE 12 TRIALS OF LUCY

TRIAL 5

3D Head Mushroom Project

As Heracles redirected rivers to cleanse the Augean stables, you must now harness modern technology and natural processes to create something both peculiar and fascinating.

Your Task: 3D scan a parent's head, refine in Blender, 3D print at 50% scale, fill with mushroom substrate, and create a time-lapse of enoki mushrooms growing from the eyes and moth (and maybe ears). The video must be edited and uploaded like trial 4.

This challenge combines the ancient virtue of innovation with modern technology. Just as Heracles thought outside the expected methods to clean the stables, you must utilize digital scanning, modeling, printing, and cultivation techniques in unexpected ways.

The Greeks revered both Hephaestus, god of craftsmanship and technology, and Dionysus, god of vegetation and rebirth. This trial honors both through its combination of technical skill and natural growth.

Cleaning the Augean stables required Heracles to think creatively, using the power of flowing rivers rather than conventional tools. Similarly, this trial asks you to merge technology with nature in a creative solution. The 3D scanning represents capturing reality, much as Heracles had to assess the immense task before him. The growing mushrooms symbolize how something new and useful can emerge from what appears strange or unusual - just as the stables, once cleaned, became valuable land again.
