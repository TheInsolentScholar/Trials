THE 12 TRIALS OF LUCY

TRIAL 9

Build a World (A Small One)

As Heracles journeyed to the land of the Amazons to retrieve the magical belt of Queen Hippolyta, you must now venture into the realm of digital creation to build a world of your own design.

Your Task: Build an interactive world in Unreal Engine. It can be any size you want and must include a building, some water, a room that contains the scanned head from Trial 5, and a player must be able to move around in it in 1st or 3rd person.

The Greeks believed that the gods themselves had shaped the world with divine power. Demiurges and creator deities were central to many ancient cosmologies. Now you shall harness the power of modern technology to become a creator in your own right.

This challenge tests your ability to envision a space and bring it to life using digital tools. Your world must be both aesthetically pleasing and functional, allowing exploration and interaction.

When Heracles sought the belt of Hippolyta, he traveled to a distant and exotic land, ruled by warrior women with their own culture and architecture. Similarly, you must create a unique environment with its own aesthetic and character. Just as Heracles had to navigate this unfamiliar realm, others will navigate the digital world you create. The inclusion of the scanned head from Trial 5 represents bringing back a "trophy" from your previous adventures, much as Heracles was tasked with retrieving the belt.
