THE 12 TRIALS OF LUCY

TRIAL 7

Enter Sandman Guitar Intro

As Heracles tamed the wild Cretan Bull, bringing harmony to chaos, so must you master the Stratocaster's strings to produce one of rock's most iconic melodies.

Your Task: Learn and demonstrate proficiency playing the iconic opening section of Metallica's "Enter Sandman" on the Stratocaster guitar.

The ancient Greeks revered music as a divine art. Apollo, god of music, was said to play the lyre with such skill that even the gods wept. Now you must channel your inner Apollo to master this modern instrument.

Like the bull that Heracles had to capture and control without harming it, the guitar requires firm but precise handling. Too much force ruins the sound; too little fails to produce it. Finding this balance requires patience and practice.

When Heracles captured the Cretan Bull, he had to demonstrate both strength and finesse - subduing the powerful creature without causing harm. Similarly, mastering the guitar requires both strength (to press the strings firmly) and finesse (to create clean notes and transitions). The famous Enter Sandman riff, like the bull itself, may seem intimidating at first, but with practice and determination, it can be tamed into submission.
